## About **"Pink Cucumbers"**  
Welcome to the Pink Cucumbers project.
This project aims to teach everyone how to cook this incredible dish. What you do with it is your choice...

## Installing Pink Cucumbers

### List of Ingredients
- 2 Green Cucumbers
- 5 pounds of happy grass
- 75 vials of magic
- 1 salt shaker half filled with fairy dust
- 1 bottle of Pink Unicorn Juice

### Launching the Dish
1. **Clone this Repository and Initialize the Culinary IDE:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/CucumberCoders/PinkCucumbers.git
   cd PinkCucumbers
2. **Prep the Cucumbers:**
   - Dice the Green Cucumbers into psychedelic shapes. Think irregular polygons, not your average cubes.

3. **Infuse the Happy Grass:**
   - Sprinkle the 5 pounds of happy grass liberally over the cucumbers. Let the happiness soak in.

4. **Conjure the Magic:**
   - Uncork the 75 vials of magic and pour them over the cucumbers. 

5. **Shake the Fairy Dust Salt Bae Style:**
   - Holding the salt shaker half filled with fairy dust, perform the sacred Salt Bae ritual over the cucumbers. This step requires style; don't hold back.

6. **Unicorn Magic Elixir:**
   - Finally, pour the Bottle of Pink Unicorn Juice with the finesse of a mixologist. Watch as the cucumbers transform into a kaleidoscope of flavors.

7. Run the script and watch the green cucumbers transform into dazzling pink beauties, fear not. They're just culinary companions on this bizarre journey.
